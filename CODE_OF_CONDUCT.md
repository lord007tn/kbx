# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We aim to make kbx a respectful, focused, and useful open-source project. Contributors and maintainers are expected to communicate clearly, assume good intent, and avoid harassment, insults, or personal attacks.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Keep technical disagreement focused on evidence, tradeoffs, and user impact.
- Accept maintainer decisions about scope, timing, and project direction.
- Report problems privately when they involve security or personal safety.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Trolling, repeated off-topic comments, or bad-faith participation.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Publicly disclosing security vulnerabilities before maintainers can respond.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues or PRs; or block participants who violate this code. Serious or repeated violations may be reported to GitHub.
